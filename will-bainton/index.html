About.

Hi, I’m Will. I design simple and beautiful things. I’m a freelance Web Developer with over two years of experience problem-solving and creating with code.

Since I began programming, I’ve attempted to apply what I have learned in my lifetime to each of my projects, by marrying my goals with my intuition and my experience. My hope is to construct things that are as easy to use as they are effective at what they do. I think they should also look nice.

Work

This selection of my projects represents two of my main interests. The first is a love for creating intuitive solutions to difficult problems in daily life, by trying to bridge the gap between what someone wants to do with their time, and their ability to do it. The second is a desire for simple fun an entertainment, seen in my love for puzzles and games.

Why

Each of these recent works is the seed of something larger - the start of a project that I want to realize on a larger scale. As a whole, I would like to make work that makes the difficult tasks easier, and the entertaining things more fun. As I continue to grow and develop, so too does my curiosity for just what makes something useful, and what makes it great.
